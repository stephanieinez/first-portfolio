About

I'm Steph, a Berlin-based ex-journalist, communications manager and marketer from Australia. I've recently found myself in the exciting world of web development! I'm currently doing a frontend apprenticeship at HelloFresh - working in React.js, Redux and JavaScript.

I hold a B.A. in International Studies, a post-graduate diploma in Journalism and five years of industry experience in London and Berlin.

Aside from programming, I write web copy, press releases and media kits. Have a look at this one I made for IDAGIO or this one I made for London-based design gallery Eiger.

I can competently use Wordpress, Medium, Mailchimp, Github, Photoshop, Sketch, HTML, CSS, Google Analytics and Tag Manager.

For the past six months I have been studying the wonderful(-ly difficult) German language. I am currently at B1, aber ich werde jeden Tag besser! I love travelling and the outdoors, taking the opportunity to go exploring at any chance I get. In my spare time I enjoy making silly projects (Check out my compliments and insult generator if you are ever stuck for words), lying in the sun and going dancing.

Why not drop me a line? I'd love to hear from you!
